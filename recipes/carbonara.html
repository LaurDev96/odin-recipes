Carbonara Recipe

Description

This Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe is so rich and decadent yet so incredibly easy to make! Pasta is tossed in a delicious and easy parmesan sauce with savory pancetta. The heat from the pasta cooks the sauce making for a quick and creamy dish in no time at all!

Ingridients

  • Pancetta
  • Guanciale
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Whisk Sauce
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Toss Together
